Website Planning Document

Author: Jerson Porras

Site Name

Civic Si Tuner Hub

Site Purpose

To provide a dedicated resource for 2012 Honda Civic Si enthusiasts focusing on performance modifications and troubleshooting.

Scenarios

  • How do I address a rich fuel mixture issue with a K&N intake?
  • What is the process for fixing a parasitic battery drain from the CANBUS decoder?

Color Schema

Primary: #CC0000 (Red) - Headings and Buttons

Secondary: #1A1A1A (Black) - Backgrounds and Accents

Typography

Font: Roboto, sans-serif (Headings and Body)

Wireframes

Mobile and Desktop views follow a standard PARC design principle layout.
